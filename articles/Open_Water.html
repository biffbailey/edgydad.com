6 tips to help you improve your balance in the water

  1. Keep a neutral head position. Look down (not forward or back towards your feet).
  2. Maintain a small and continuous (constant) kick. Use the kick in concert with rotation.
  3. Keep your arms from crossing over to the opposite side.
  4. Feel your hips on top of the water by engaging your core muscles.
  5. Add weights to your training program. Focus on core strength and rotational movements.
  6. Just relax. “The water is your friend. You don’t have to fight with water; just share the same spirit as the water, and it will help you move.” – Alexander Popov, Olympic Gold Medalist
