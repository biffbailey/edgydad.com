Ingredients

  • 2/3 cups warm water (105 to 110 degrees F)
  • 1/4 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 tablespoons olive or canola oil, plus more for bowl

Instructions

  1. Proof the yeast by sprinkling on top of water.
  2. Let rest for about 10 minutes until yeast gets bubbly and foamy.
  3. Add water and yeast to the Processor Bowl.
  4. Add flour, sugar, salt and ¼ tablespoon of oil.
  5. (Pulse and hold to) blend and continue processing until dough forms a ball.
  6. Continue to process to knead the dough until dough is smooth and elastic.
  7. Oil a large bowl and turn out dough into bowl.
  8. Cover with a tea towel and let rise until doubled in size.
  9. Roll and stretch into pizza pan
  10. Cook at 425℉ for 8 to 10 minutes
  11. Add toppings and bake for additional 8 to 10 minutes
